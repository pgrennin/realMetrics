      Capitilaziation Rate
      The capitalization rate is the rate of return on a real estate investment property based on the income that the property is expected to generate.
      Net Operating Income
      Net operating income, or NOI, is the sum of all positive cash flows from rents and other sources of ordinary income generated by a property, minus the sum of ongoing expenses, such as maintenance, utilities, fees, taxes, and other items of that nature.
